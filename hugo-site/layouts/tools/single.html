About PsychoPharmRef

PsychoPharmRef is a free clinical psychopharmacology reference built by Jerad Shoemaker, MD for psychiatrists, residents, and medical students. All tools are evidence-based and designed for point-of-care clinical decision support.

  • 91 psychiatric medications with complete pharmacology data
  • 15+ interactive clinical rating scales (PANSS, SLUMS, CDR, PCL-5, Y-BOCS, YMRS, BFCRS, AIMS, and more)
  • Receptor binding profiles with bar chart and treemap visualization
  • PK curve comparison tool with Bateman equation modeling
  • P450 drug interaction checker
  • QTc calculator with Tisdale risk scoring
  • Medication taper and cross-taper planners
  • 68 evidence-based clinical blog posts
