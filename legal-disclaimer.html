Not a Substitute for Clinical Judgment

Nothing in this tool constitutes medical advice, a clinical recommendation, or a standard of care. All information must be interpreted within the full context of an individual patient's clinical presentation, history, and current medications by a qualified clinician.

Data Accuracy & Currency

  • Data is derived from FDA-approved prescribing information, the PDSP Ki database, and peer-reviewed pharmacology literature. It may not reflect the most current labeling, post-market safety updates, or newly approved indications.
  • Receptor binding values (Ki) are sourced from in vitro studies and may not directly predict in vivo clinical effects at therapeutic doses.
  • The QT risk tool, Tisdale score, and all rating-scale tools are provided for educational awareness only and do not replace a full clinical assessment, real-time ECG interpretation, or structured diagnostic interview.

Scope of Use

  • This reference does not account for individual patient factors including renal or hepatic impairment, genetic polymorphisms, pregnancy, pediatric populations, or off-label indications unless explicitly noted.
  • Drug interaction screening presented here is not comprehensive. Always verify interactions using a current clinical decision support system (e.g., Lexicomp, Micromedex, Clinical Pharmacology).
  • Always consult current FDA-approved labeling and applicable clinical practice guidelines before making any prescribing or clinical decision.
